## NOTES FOR TRANSLATORS

### Malagasy Language Notes:

1. **Formatting:**
   - Use standard Malagasy spelling
   - Avoid French words unless technical terms
   - Use appropriate noun prefixes (ny, i, etc.)

2. **Technical Terms:**
   - "Rano" = water (noun)
   - "Taolan'rano" = water reservoir
   - "Fandrefesana" = measurement/sensor
   - "Famantarana" = monitoring/alert
   - "Hafanana" = temperature
   - "Mangatsiaka" = humidity

3. **Capitalization:**
   - Capitalize first word of sentences
   - Keep component names (ESP32, DHT22) as-is

4. **Abbreviations:**
   - LCD → LCD (same in Malagasy)
   - LED → LED (same in Malagasy)
   - pH → pH (same in Malagasy)
